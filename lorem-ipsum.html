What is lorem ipsum?

      
Lorem ipsum is scrambled, Latin-looking placeholder text that designers and developers drop into a layout before the real copy is ready. Because the words carry no meaning, they let you judge typography, spacing, and visual balance without being distracted by what the text says. The passage is derived from a work by Cicero written more than two thousand years ago, with letters and words altered so it reads as nonsense.

      
How to use it

      
1. Set Count to how much text you need. 2. Choose a Type — paragraphs, sentences, or words. 3. Leave Start with "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet…" ticked if you want the traditional opening, or untick it for fully random output. 4. Click Generate, then Copy to grab the result.

      
Why use placeholder text?

      
Filler text keeps a mock-up neutral. Real sentences pull the eye toward their content and tempt stakeholders to edit copy during a design review; meaningless dummy text keeps everyone focused on the layout itself. It is also handy for testing how a component, table, or paragraph block behaves with realistic-length content before the final words arrive.

      
Frequently asked questions

      
Is the generated text really random? Each sentence is assembled from a classic lorem ipsum word bank with a randomly varied length, so no two runs are identical. With the start option enabled, the very first sentence always begins with the familiar "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet" line.

      
Can I generate just a few words or a whole article? Yes. Pick Words for a short snippet, Sentences for a mid-length block, or Paragraphs for long-form filler. The count accepts any value from 1 to 200.

      
Is my data sent anywhere? No. This lorem ipsum generator runs entirely in your browser. Nothing you generate is ever uploaded to a server.
