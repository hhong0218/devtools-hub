Contact

DevTools Hub is a small, independently run project, and we genuinely read every message. Whether something is broken, you have an idea for a tool, or you just want to say hello, we'd like to hear from you.

Email us

The best way to reach us is by email:

infomatch@gmail.com

What to write about

  • Bug reports — if a tool gives a wrong result or won't load, tell us which tool and, if you can, what you entered (only if it isn't sensitive) and which browser you're using.
  • Tool requests — is there a developer utility you keep wishing were here? Suggest it and we'll consider adding it.
  • Feedback — anything that would make the site clearer, faster, or more useful.
  • Corrections — spotted a mistake in one of our guides? Let us know and we'll fix it.

Response time

We usually reply within a few days. There's no automated ticketing system on the other end — just a person reading your note, so a little detail goes a long way.

Privacy

All tools on DevTools Hub run entirely in your browser and never upload what you enter. If you email us, we only see what you choose to include in your message. See our Privacy Policy for more.
